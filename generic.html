Comprehensive Research and Analysis Reports

Authored by Peter Gatitu Mwangi, these reports explore key socio-economic and business trends in Kenya, providing valuable insights and actionable recommendations for various stakeholders. Each report delves deeply into its subject, offering data-driven perspectives to guide decisions and strategies for sustainable growth.

Analyzing Agricultural and Livestock Production Trends in Kenya (2019-2023)

This report provides a detailed analysis of Kenya's agricultural and livestock production trends between 2019 and 2023. It covers major sectors such as cereals, horticulture, industrial crops, and livestock, highlighting the challenges posed by climate variability, market demands, and policy decisions. Using data analytics, Peter Gatitu Mwangi identifies key patterns and forecasts production trends for 2024, offering actionable insights to address these challenges and enhance agricultural output. The findings emphasize the importance of sustainable practices and innovative solutions to ensure food security and economic stability.

Mobile Money as a Driver of Kenya's Economic Development

This study examines the transformative impact of mobile money on Kenya's economic development from 2019 to 2023. Focusing on its role in financial inclusion, credit expansion, and GDP growth, the report explores the dynamic relationship between mobile money transactions, domestic credit, and economic performance. Author Peter Gatitu Mwangi uses trend and elasticity analyses to reveal the profound contributions of mobile financial services in reshaping Kenya's economy. Recommendations include investing in infrastructure, promoting regulatory frameworks, and expanding access to mobile money to harness its full potential for inclusive growth.

Sales Performance Analysis: Identifying Key Drivers and Customer Trends

This report delves into transaction data to uncover the drivers of sales performance in Kenya. By analyzing customer segmentation, brand trends, and life-stage group preferences, Peter Gatitu Mwangi identifies the most lucrative customer segments and product categories. Key findings include the dominance of premium customers and specific life-stage groups such as young families and older singles/couples. The report provides actionable strategies, such as targeted marketing and strategic brand partnerships, to optimize business performance and maximize revenue.

Unemployment Trends and Analysis from 1991 to 2023

This comprehensive study explores unemployment trends in Kenya over three decades, emphasizing the effects of major economic disruptions such as the 2008 global financial crisis and the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic. Authored by Peter Gatitu Mwangi, the report examines the resilience of labor markets and the effectiveness of government interventions during these periods. Through data-driven insights, the report highlights recovery patterns, long-term changes in labor dynamics, and the need for proactive policies to minimize unemployment volatility. Recommendations include strengthening social safety nets, investing in workforce retraining, and implementing job creation initiatives.
